The user has the ability to:<br/><ol>
<li>Conduct a conversation in two ways:</li></ol>
<ul>
<li>Through text chat,</li><li>Through video call.</li></ul>
2.    Express additional reactions during a text chat by:<br/><ul>
<li>Sending a GIF,</li><li>Sending a Facebook sticker.</li></ul>
3.    Delete the text chat (which is equivalent to removing the matched profile)<br/>
